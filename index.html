The Game
A living world of land, strategy and economy

Luminaea is a 320-unit hex territory on Mina Protocol — the world's lightest blockchain. Players buy, develop and trade land units using $ARKIS, an in-game token built on Mina's custom token standard. Each territory is governed by a Minalien NFT minister with unique traits that affect the land. Tune your units weekly, build developments, win auctions, and rise to dominance across 20 districts — all verifiable on-chain with zero-knowledge proofs that protect player privacy.

🗄️ Decentralised Database & IPFS Storage

MINALIA currently uses Supabase (PostgreSQL) as its backend — a pragmatic choice for rapid development. The long-term roadmap investigates migrating to a decentralised database layer, eliminating any single point of control over game data.

TOP CANDIDATE
Tableland
Decentralised SQLite — SQL queries on-chain, wallet-based access control. Tables are ERC-721 tokens. Backed by Filecoin/IPFS. Most similar to our existing relational structure.
STRONG OPTION
OrbitDB
Serverless peer-to-peer database built directly on IPFS. Uses Merkle-CRDTs for conflict-free sync across nodes. No central server — data lives with the community.
ZK-NATIVE
Polybase
Uses ZK-rollup proofs to enforce data rules — aligns directly with MINALIA's ZK-first philosophy. Data writes are governed by Polylang functions, not a central authority.

Alongside decentralised DB, game assets — NFT metadata, artwork, territory records — will be stored on IPFS (InterPlanetary File System), ensuring no single server controls access to player-owned content. Files stored on IPFS are content-addressed — meaning the data can never be silently altered or deleted, giving players genuine ownership of their assets in perpetuity.

Planned ZK Proof Integrations
🌱
Proof of First Login
Mina timestamp · Nullifier
CONCEPT

Prove your wallet connected to Luminaea before a specific block height — without revealing when exactly. Earns the Pioneer badge, which is non-transferable and permanently attached to the wallet.

Real-world analogy: Proving you were an early adopter of a protocol without revealing your exact join date or activity level.
💰
Proof of Funds (Range Proof)
o1js Field comparison · Range check
CONCEPT

Prove a wallet holds ≥ X $ARKIS without revealing the exact balance. Used for bid eligibility thresholds, loan qualification, and territory access gates — keeping wealth private.

Real-world analogy: Mortgage pre-approval that proves sufficient funds without exposing bank statements to a third party.
🪪
Proof of Unique Identity
zkPassport · Nullifier · One-per-human
CONCEPT

One human = one account. Using zkPassport or a Worldcoin-style nullifier, prove you are a unique person without revealing any personal data. Critical for fair play and preventing multi-wallet abuse.

Real-world analogy: Voter registration — prove eligibility and uniqueness without revealing personal identity to the registrar.
🏠
Proof of Ownership
Merkle witness · On-chain state
CONCEPT

Prove ownership of N units held for ≥ T days without revealing which specific units or the exact count. Unlocks governance voting weight, tax exemptions and VIP territory access.

Real-world analogy: Proving long-term property residency for council voting rights without revealing your address or property value.
📊
Proof of Trade History
Recursive proof · Merkle accumulator
CONCEPT

Prove you've completed ≥ N trades at ≥ X ARKIS total volume without revealing counterparties, prices or timing. Builds a merchant reputation score that's verifiable but private.

Real-world analogy: Credit score that proves creditworthiness without sharing individual transaction history with lenders.
👑
Proof of Territory Dominance
Threshold proof · Aggregate state
CONCEPT

Prove majority stake in a territory (≥ 9 of 16 units) without revealing exact ownership count or which units are held. Unlocks Minister-level governance and treasury control.

Real-world analogy: Proving controlling interest in a company for board voting rights without disclosing exact shareholding percentage.
🏗️
Proof of Development Milestone
State transition proof · Time-lock
CONCEPT

Prove a unit has had a Level 3+ building for ≥ 30 Luminaea days without exposing full build history. Awards prestige badges to long-term builders, rewarding patience over speculation.

Real-world analogy: Proving continuous business operation for a trading licence without exposing full financial records.
🎵
Proof of Score Threshold
ZK-SNARK · Committed value comparison
CONCEPT

Prove your tune score average is above a threshold without revealing individual scores. Enables leaderboard eligibility and reward tiers where the actual scores remain private to the player.

Real-world analogy: Proving exam pass without revealing your mark — the institution knows you passed, not by how much.
🤝
Proof of DAO Contribution (Multi-party)
Multi-party ZK · Contribution nullifier · Recursive o1js
CONCEPT

Prove you contributed to ≥ 2 Nexus building collaborations — where each collaborator proves their individual share privately, and a recursive proof aggregates contributions without any party revealing their exact input. This is a multi-party ZK construction: the most technically ambitious proof on this roadmap and a direct demonstration of o1js recursive proof composition.

Real-world analogy: A joint venture where each partner proves their capital contribution to a threshold without any partner seeing the others' amounts — enabling trustless co-investment.

Deep Game Mechanics Roadmap
Engagement · Retention · Community
ROADMAP

The current game is a foundation. Luminaea is the first of 5 planned territories that launch sequentially — each new territory expanding the player base, unlocking new mechanics and deepening the economy. As territories open, so does the scope: alliances, cross-territory diplomacy, resource trading markets, minister elections, AI-driven world events, and player-governed treasuries. The full game is designed for several thousand players across all territories, with each layer of complexity unlocking only once the community has grown into it. Every decision matters not just for your own land, but for the living world around it.

Why it matters for Mina: Every engaged player is a long-term holder of Mina, an Auro wallet user, and a potential advocate for the protocol — turning game growth directly into ecosystem growth. 5 territories means 5 sequential on-chain launches, each one a new event for the Mina community. MINALIA also serves as a visible, positive showcase for o1 Labs and the wider Mina ecosystem — demonstrating that real, engaging products can be built on zero-knowledge infrastructure. Each milestone creates ripples into the broader ZK space, attracting new builders, new ideas and new energy to Mina.

Mina Web Node Integration
Mina Web Node (in development) · Light client · Network participation
FUTURE

As Mina's Web Node matures, MINALIA aims to embed it directly into the game client. Players who opt in would run a lightweight Mina node inside their browser while they play — contributing to network security and decentralisation without any additional setup. The game becomes not just a use case for Mina, but an active participant in keeping the network healthy. Playing Luminaea would mean helping secure the world's lightest blockchain at the same time.

Why it matters: Distributing node participation across thousands of engaged game players dramatically strengthens Mina's decentralisation — and gives players a tangible sense that their presence in the game has real-world impact beyond the screen.
