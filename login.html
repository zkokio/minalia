The Chronicles of Etheria
Before Luminaea
Long ago, on the vibrant and harmonious planet Etheria, the ancestors of Baby Minaliens faced an impossible choice.
Their twin suns, once the source of life and stability, had begun their catastrophic dance toward destruction.
The wisest of their kind, the Elders of Etheria, devised the Ark Initiative — a plan to send their youngest and brightest to a new world.
A planet discovered in the distant reaches of space, similar to their own — a civilisation on the verge of extinction reaching out across the stars.
The Ark Initiative
A Civilisation Encoded in Light
The Ark Initiative was their final gambit — a civilisation encoded into light, launched across the void.
The Arks were no mere ships; they were
hallowed sanctuaries of stasis.
By weaving Vyralon — the crystalline life-blood of Etheria's core — into the very air, the Elders suspended the touch of time itself.
Inside each Ark, the Elders created Knowledge Chambers — crystalline, biomechanical spaces infused with the essence of Etherian wisdom.
The Knowledge Chambers
Learning Through Light
Instead of books or lectures, the chambers used liquid light pools — viscous, glowing substances the Baby Minaliens could absorb through touch, sight, and even taste.
When they dipped their hands into the pools, the liquid light flowed into them, embedding knowledge directly into their minds.
History, science, art, survival skills — everything the Elders knew became part of them, not as dry facts, but as vivid, immersive experiences.
The Voyage
Frozen in Dawn
The chambers were interactive environments where the travellers could consume knowledge in a way that felt natural to their playful and inquisitive nature.
Within this glowing embrace, the Baby Minaliens remained unaged — their hearts and bodies frozen in a dawn that never ended.
The Elders' Legacy
Ageless Pioneers
This eternal youth was the Elders' ultimate legacy. They sought to bottle the boundless wonder and tireless creativity of childhood, shielding it from the decay of years.
They dreamed that when these voyagers finally touched a new shore, they would step forth not as weary survivors, but as ageless pioneers.
Possessing the innocence of the young and the tempered wisdom of the stars.
The Ark Initiative · Year 27
A New World Awaits
The Elders selected a small group of Baby Minaliens to govern each territory on the planet — the Ministers.
After 27 years of travel, the Arks reached a new world — Luminaea.
You are one of the first to arrive. You have been chosen to help the Ministers create a brave new world.
Your decisions will shape what Luminaea becomes.
