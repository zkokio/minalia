📖 How It Works
⚖️
Exchange Rates
All tokens are priced relative to $ARKIS (rate = 1). Rates reflect each token's yield value and scarcity. SPORE is rarest and most valuable per unit — PLASM is most abundant.
🔥
2% Fee — Burned
Every trade incurs a 2% fee on the output token. This fee is permanently burned — removed from total supply forever. Burning creates mild deflation and increasing scarcity over time.
📅
Daily Limit
Each player can make up to 5 trades per day. This prevents arbitrage farming and keeps rates stable. Resets at midnight UTC each day.
🌀
Dual-Direction
Enter an amount on either side. Type how much you want to receive and the widget back-calculates exactly how much to send, including the fee.
🏗
Why Exchange?
Special buildings require specific tokens. A WIRE building can't be paid in PLASM. Exchange lets you convert whatever you've earned into what you need to unlock the build you want.
🔒
Secure
All exchanges run as a single atomic DB transaction. Rates are fetched server-side — client values are never trusted. Balance is checked before any debit. No partial fills possible.
